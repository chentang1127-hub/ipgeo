Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

IPGeo processes IP addresses solely for the purpose of providing geolocation lookups. We do not log or store the IP addresses queried through the API after the lookup is complete.

2. Account Information

When you sign up, we collect and store:

  • Email address (for account management and billing notifications)
  • API key (hashed with SHA-256; plaintext key is shown only once at creation)
  • Usage metrics (query count for billing purposes)

3. Payment Information

All payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card or payment details. Paddle's privacy policy applies to all payment data.

4. Cookies

The IPGeo API itself does not use cookies. The documentation website may use minimal cookies for functionality (e.g., API console preferences). No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregate usage statistics publicly (e.g., "X billion lookups served").

6. Data Security

API keys are stored as SHA-256 hashes. All API traffic should use HTTPS. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your account information.

7. Data Retention

Account information is retained while your account is active. Upon account closure, your data is deleted within 30 days. Billing records are retained as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may export your usage data at any time via the API. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@getipgeo.com.

9. Third-Party Services

Our Service uses MaxMind GeoLite2 databases under the CC BY-SA 4.0 license. IPGeo itself is not affiliated with MaxMind.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email to account holders. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@getipgeo.com
