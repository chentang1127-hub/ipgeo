Last updated: June 14, 2026

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee
If you're not satisfied with IPGeo for any reason, request a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. No questions asked.

1. Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Monthly plans: Full refund if requested within 7 days of the first charge for a new subscription.
  • Annual plans: Full refund within 7 days; prorated refund thereafter.
  • Prepaid credits: Refundable within 7 days if unused. After 7 days, credits are non-refundable but never expire.
  • Service outage: If uptime falls below 99% in a given month, you may request a credit proportional to the downtime.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • Renewal charges beyond the 7-day window
  • Usage already consumed at the time of refund request
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Chargebacks (please contact us first — we'll resolve it faster)

3. How to Request a Refund

Send an email to billing@ipgeo.io with:

  • Your account email
  • The transaction date and amount
  • Reason for refund (optional but appreciated)

We process refunds within 5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Paddle.

4. Chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us at billing@ipgeo.io before initiating a chargeback. Most billing issues can be resolved within 24 hours.

5. Contact

Billing inquiries: billing@ipgeo.io
